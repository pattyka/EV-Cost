# [EV Cost](https://evcost.hu)  
**Track and compare the cost of driving electric and traditional vehicles with ease.**

---

## 🚗 About  
[EV Cost](https://evcost.hu) is a sleek and minimalist app designed to help you calculate and compare the cost of driving an electric vehicle (EV) versus an internal combustion engine (ICE) car. With its intuitive interface and customizable features, EV Cost is perfect for eco-conscious drivers and cost-savvy car enthusiasts alike.

---

## ✨ Features  

### 📊 **Interactive Charts**  
- Visualize the cost difference between your EV and ICE vehicle with clear, engaging charts.  
- Easy-to-read data helps you make smarter driving decisions.  

### 💶 **Currency Switching**  
- Seamlessly toggle between **HUF** and **EUR** for accurate cost calculations, no matter your location.

### 🔧 **Customizable Inputs**  
- Personalize key values to suit your specific vehicle and energy prices:  
  - **EV energy consumption** (default: 14 kWh/100 km)  
  - **ICE fuel consumption** (default: 5.2 L/100 km)  
  - **Electricity cost** (default: 70 HUF/kWh)  
  - **Fuel price** (default: 590 HUF/L)  

### 🖤 **Minimalist Design**  
- A clean, Apple-inspired interface.  
- Built with a focus on simplicity and efficiency, making it a joy to use.
- Also supports dark mode, so your eyes dont burn out from all of those savings!

### 👨‍💻 **User-Friendly Experience**  
- Intuitive navigation and easy-to-understand tools.  
- Haptic feedback for a polished, tactile experience.
